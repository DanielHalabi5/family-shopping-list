Theme:

Background (Signup/Login):
Soft blue–indigo gradient → calm, trustworthy, and modern first impression.

Nav Buttons:
gray → clean, minimal look; whitens on active for feedback.

Confirm/Submit Buttons:
Deep blue → emphasizes trust and importance; darker on hover for action clarity.

Input Fields:
Light gray borders + blue focus ring → simple, friendly, and accessible design.

Create Family Section:
Light blue tones → symbolizes reliability and guidance.

Join Family Section:
Amber tones → represents warmth, connection, and friendliness.

Overall Feel:
Balanced mix of trust (blue), warmth (amber), and clean modern design (white/gray) → ideal for a family-oriented and user-friendly interface.
